Colin Baker

Colin Baker (born 8 June 1943) is an English actor, who is known as Paul Merroney in The Brothers from 1974 to 1976

and as the sixth incarnation of  The Doctor in the long-running science fiction television series Doctor Who from 1984 to 1986.
