Arthur Darvill

Thomas Arthur Darvill (born 17 June 1982) is an English actor and singer, known professionally as Arthur Darvill.[1] He is noted for his work in the plays Terre Haute (2006) and Swimming with Sharks (2007), but is best known for his role as Rory Williams, one of the Eleventh Doctor's companions in the television series Doctor Who
